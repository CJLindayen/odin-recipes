Bismuth Crystals

Description:

Make your own gorgeous bismuth crystal at home! A beautiful display and easy science experiment.

Materials:

  • 50 g solid bismuth ingots
  • Ladle
  • Pre-heated ceramic bowl or pot

Procedure:

  1. Place the ingots in a ladle and position the ladle over a stove flame.
  2. Heat on high for 2-5 miutes until the bismuth fully melts.
  3. Slightly rock the ladle to observe the ripples on the surface of the liquid. Once the crystals begin to grow and the ripples subside, pour out the remaining liquid in a pre-heated waste container.
  4. Allow enough time for the ladle to cool before attempting to remove the crystal.
