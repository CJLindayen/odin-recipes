Vanilla Ice Cream

Description:

Homemade vanilla ice cream that can double as a fun science project! Teach thermodynamics with a tasty result.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbs sugar
  • 1/2 cup salt
  • 1/2 cup half-and-half
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 cups ice cubes

Steps:

  1. In a small zip-lock bag, place the sugar, half-and-half, and vanilla extract. Seal the bag well.
  2. Add the ice cubes and salt to a large zip-lock bag.
  3. Put the small bag inside of the large bag. Ensure that both bags are sealed well.
  4. Wrap the bag in a small towel and shake it for five minutes. Check the small bag every couple of minutes for solidification.
