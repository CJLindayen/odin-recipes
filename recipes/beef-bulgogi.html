Beef Bulgogi

Description:

Rich beef bulgogi with a flavorful marinade. A wonderful main dish or a great addition to our corn cheese recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 5 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion
  • 2 1/2 tbsp white sugar
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 lb flank steak, thinly sliced

Steps:

  1. Whisk soy sauce, green onion, sugar, garlic, sesame seeds, sesame oil, and pepper together in a bowl.
  2. Place flank steak slices in a shallow dish. Pour marinade over top. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, and lightly oil the grate.
  4. Quickly grill flank steak slices on the preheated grill until slightly charred and cooked through, 1-2 minutes per side.
